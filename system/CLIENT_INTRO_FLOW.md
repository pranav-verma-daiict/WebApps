# Client Acquisition Flow

## Strategy
Do NOT sell websites.
Sell "online presence improvement".

---

## Cold approach message

Hi! I noticed your business doesn’t have a proper website.

I built a simple example showing how your online presence could look.

Would you like me to send it?

---

## If they respond YES

Send demo + say:

I can build a full version for your business in a few days with:
- mobile-friendly website
- Google Maps integration
- contact form
- fast loading design

---

## Pricing strategy

Start low:
- 5,000–8,000 SEK initial clients

Then increase after portfolio grows.

---

## Key principle
Show before you sell.
